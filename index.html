About Us

QIncubate is a Kenyan-based startup health systems consulting firm incorporated in 2024 to strengthen health systems by developing practical solutions that enhance availability and accessibility of quality, cost effective healthcare.

Our Vision

To pioneer continous quality improvement frameworks to promote positive health outcomes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to contribute to value-based healthcare by harnessing the power of continuous quality improvement and sparking quality consciousness in developing countries.
